Erasable Inc. is University of Maryland’s only all-improv theatre group. We are dedicated to the art and fun of improv. We perform every Friday rain or shine on the steps of McKeldin Library. We started in the spring of 1986 with the help of Yale’s Purple Crayon, and ever since we’ve been going strong, with weekly improv shows for as long as we can remember: Fridays at 1 PM, steps of McKeldin Library.
